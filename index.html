About Us

Built on
Reliability.

On Time Construction was founded in 2000 by Johnny Barela, a licensed New Mexico contractor with a simple but powerful philosophy: show up on time, do the job right, and keep the customer's vision at the center of every decision.


Based in Albuquerque's northeast heights, we serve homeowners and businesses across the greater Albuquerque metro. Specializing in interior and exterior countertops, kitchen and bathroom remodeling, and full construction projects, we bring over 25 years of craftsmanship to every job — big or small.
